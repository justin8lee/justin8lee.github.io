Music & Guitar

I’ve played guitar for more than a decade, focusing on tone, timing, and phrasing. My style blends rock, blues, and contemporary improvisation, shaped by a goal of clarity and control rather than speed or flash.

I teach private lessons to help players develop fundamentals in tone shaping, chord fluency, and rhythm. I believe progress happens when technique and musical awareness grow together.

Focus Areas

  • Clean phrasing and tone control
  • Chord fluency and rhythm precision
  • Improvisation over practical harmony
  • Efficient practice structure

You can find short clips and performances on Instagram: @justin8music.
