Welcome to My Website

I’m Justin Lee, a Business & Data Science student at Northeastern University in Boston. Born in Hong Kong and shaped by time in Australia, America, and Singapore, I bring a global perspective to how I learn, collaborate, and build. I enjoy translating complex questions into structured, testable ideas—then turning those insights into clear action for product, finance, or operations.

In my academic work, I focus on the intersection of analytics and decision-making: how to define the right metrics, design honest baselines, and communicate results so teams actually use them. I’m comfortable moving between data wrangling, lightweight tooling, and story-driven presentations. Beyond class, I stay curious through projects that mix code, design, and experimentation—small dashboards, cap-table models, and utility scripts that reduce friction.

Music keeps me balanced. I’m a lead guitarist and private tutor who cares about tone, touch, and timing. The practice approach I use for music—clarify goals, tighten feedback loops, and respect fundamentals—mirrors how I work in data and product. This site shares a snapshot of what I’m exploring and what I’m building next. If anything resonates, feel free to reach out.
