Welcome to My Website

I’m Justin Lee, a Business and Data Science student at Northeastern University in Boston. I’m interested in how data, clear communication, and simple tools help people make better decisions. My projects range from building lightweight dashboards to modeling financial scenarios and designing concise visuals. I focus on practical outcomes: define a question, collect what matters, present it clearly, and make the next step obvious.

I grew up across Hong Kong, Australia, the United States, and Singapore. That background shapes how I approach teamwork and problem solving—listen first, organize assumptions, and keep solutions grounded. In class and on projects, I like to turn vague ideas into structured plans with checkpoints. I enjoy work that blends analysis and design, whether that’s clarifying metrics, simplifying a chart, or writing a short note that removes confusion for others.

Outside academics, I play guitar and snowboard. Both keep me consistent: small, repeatable practice adds up over time. This site gives a brief look at what I’m doing and where I’m learning. The second page highlights music—how I think about tone, timing, and teaching. If you want to compare notes or collaborate, please reach out through the contact section below.
