Welcome

I’m Justin Lee, a Business and Data Science student at Northeastern University in Boston. My work focuses on the intersection of data, design, and decision-making — applying analytics and visualization to improve products, performance, and communication.

I enjoy working on projects that combine structure with creativity — whether building dashboards, refining financial models, or exploring how design affects interpretation. I value clarity, precision, and practical outcomes over complexity for its own sake.

Outside of academics, I’m an active guitarist and snowboarder. Music and movement keep me grounded and remind me of the importance of balance, focus, and rhythm — the same qualities that guide my professional work.
